The History Of Our Makers Faire.

  • 2006

    Start Of Makers Faire

    Maker Faire is an event started by Make Magazine in 2006, where thousands of innovators, inventors, and Do-It-Yourself tinkerers gather to hold a giant show and tell about all their best projects.

  • 2019

    The First CV Tech Makers Faire

    In 2019 we made our version of the Maker Faire. It is a show and tell event for all the best projects that students in our programs complete as they participate in all our wonderful programs.

  • This Year

    New and Improved

    Using the innovative talents of our wonderful students, we are having a in person Maker Faire this year! Seeing as last year we were unable to have an in person Makers Faire we are very hyped to have one this year and hope to make it the best one yet!
