The History Of Our Makers Fair.

  • 2006

    Start Of Makers Fair

    Maker Faire is an event started by Make Magazine in 2006, where thousands of innovators, inventors, and Do-It-Yourself tinkerers gather to hold a giant show and tell about all their best projects.

  • 2019

    The First CV Tech Makers Fair

    In 2019 we made our version of the Maker Faire is a show and tell event for all the best projects that students in our programs complete as they participate in all our wonderful programs.

  • This Year

    New and Improved

    using the innovative talents of our wonderful students, we are having a virtual Maker Faire this year. In the future, we will continue to utilize technology to show off what students accomplish here at Canadian Valley, but we also hope to have a face -to-face event so community members can interact with the projects.
